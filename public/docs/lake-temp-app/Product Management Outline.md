## 🧩 Problem & Opportunity

**Goal:** Show you understand _what_ you're solving and _why it matters_

**Include:**

- Problem statement
	- Swimming in Lake Michigan during warm months, is a quintessential Chicago experience. There isn't a day in the summer that the coasts of Lake Michigan aren't brimming with swimmers taking advantage of the cool lake. If you are familiar with Chicago weather, you know how quickly the weather can change on a dime. So when the weather starts to get warmer and get that itch to go swimming in the lake, you want to make sure you know what you are getting into! An easy-to-use web app that tells Chicago-land swimmers what temperature the lake is, is much needed!
- Target user
	- People in Chicago who like to go swimming in the lake!
- Why this is worth solving
	- Currently, there doesn't exist a user-friendly application that allows Chicagoans to access the temperate of the lake. Even when you google "lake michigan water temp" it takes a few more clicks to find the answer. Eventually, most users will end up at the [National Weather Service website](https://forecast.weather.gov/product.php?issuedby=lot&product=omr&site=lot), where the data is not easy to read. Sigh.
- Quick validation (your experience, light research, etc.)
