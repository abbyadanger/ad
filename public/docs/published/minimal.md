I've realized I'm a picky web designer. I change my mind a lot. I'm constantly making small changes to the website because my taste changes on a dime.

My theme and vision for the blog has always been ...... ü§ç**minimal**ü§ç

I hate bad design and even more than that, I hate complicated code. To me ... less will **always** be more.

It took me way too long to realize that most people will be reading the blog in a mobile browser, on their phone. I neglected to even think about this since I'm always developing a **BIG** screen .... and then I'm also testing on a **BIG** screen. What about the *small* screen?

So now I test on *small* and **BIG** screen.

### Before

That's when I realized I hated the way this part of the blog looked. Too much text. Too scrunched together. So unnecessary. Too much useless information.

### After
